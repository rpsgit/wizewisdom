Apple Crumble

🧂 Ingredients

  • 1½ cups All-Purpose Flour (~187 g)
  • ¾ cup White Sugar (~150 g)
  • ½ tsp Baking Powder (~2.3 g)
  • 1½ tbsp Cornstarch (~12 g)
  • ¼ tsp Cinnamon Powder
  • ⅛ tsp Salt
  • 1 Large Egg (~50 g)
  • ½ cup Butter (~113 g)
  • ¾ cup Apples, diced (~90 g)

🥣 Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven: 180°C (356°F).
  2. Prepare pan: Grease an 8x8-inch pan.
  3. Crust & topping: Combine flour, ½ cup sugar, baking powder. Add butter gradually, then egg until crumbly.
  4. Filling: Mix apples, cornstarch, cinnamon, remaining ¼ cup sugar.
  5. Assemble: Press ⅔ crumb mixture into pan, add filling, sprinkle remaining crumbs.
  6. Bake: 35–40 min until golden.
  7. Serve: Cool slightly, serve with ice cream.
