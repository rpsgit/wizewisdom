Apple Crumble

🧂 Ingredients

  • 1½ kg All-Purpose Flour
  • ¾ cup White Sugar
  • ½ tsp Baking Powder
  • 1½ tbsp Cornstarch
  • ¼ tsp Cinnamon Powder
  • ⅛ tsp Salt
  • 1 Large Egg
  • ½ cup (1 stick) Butter, cubed and slightly softened
  • ¾ cup Apples, diced

🥣 Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven: 180°C (356°F)
  2. Prepare the baking pan: Grease an 8x8-inch pan
  3. Make the crust & topping: Combine dry ingredients, add butter, then egg
  4. Prepare the filling: Mix apples, cornstarch, cinnamon, remaining sugar
  5. Assemble: Press ⅔ crumb mixture into pan, add filling, top with remaining crumbs
  6. Bake: 35–40 minutes or until golden
  7. Serve: Let cool and enjoy with ice cream
