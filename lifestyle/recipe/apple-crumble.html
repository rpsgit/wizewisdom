Apple Crumble

🧂 Ingredients

  • 1½ kg All-Purpose Flour
  • ¾ cup White Sugar
  • ½ tsp Baking Powder
  • 1½ tbsp Cornstarch
  • ¼ tsp Cinnamon Powder
  • ⅛ tsp Salt
  • 1 Large Egg
  • ½ cup (1 stick) Butter, cubed and slightly softened
  • ¾ cup Apples, diced

🥣 Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven:
    Preheat to 180°C (356°F).
  2. Prepare the baking pan:
    Grease an 8x8-inch pan with butter and set aside.
  3. Make the crust & topping:
    In a bowl, combine flour, ½ cup sugar, and baking powder. Add butter gradually while mixing, then blend in the egg until crumbly.
  4. Prepare the filling:
    In another bowl, mix diced apples, cornstarch, cinnamon, and the remaining ¼ cup sugar.
  5. Assemble:
    Press ⅔ of the crumb mixture into the pan as crust. Spread the apple filling on top, then sprinkle the remaining crumbs.
  6. Bake:
    Bake for 35–40 minutes or until golden. Ovens vary, so check occasionally.
  7. Serve:
    Let cool slightly and serve with vanilla ice cream.
