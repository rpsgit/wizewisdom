Journey of Souls

by Michael Newton, Ph.D.

Journey of Souls explores what happens between lives through hypnotherapy sessions. Newton’s findings reveal a structured “soul journey” that offers practical lessons for living with more purpose today.

Key Insights

  • Life Has Purpose: Each soul chooses its circumstances for growth. Challenges are lessons, not punishments.
  • Souls Travel in Groups: We reincarnate with familiar souls to learn together. Relationships are often part of bigger patterns.
  • Between Lives is Healing & Planning: After death, souls review, heal, and prepare for the next life—mistakes are feedback, not failure.
  • Spirit Guides Exist: We are continuously guided, often through intuition and synchronicities.
  • Levels of Soul Development: Souls advance like students in school—growth is gradual, not a race.
  • Free Will Matters: Plans exist, but choices always belong to us.

How to Use These Insights Daily

  • Mindset Shift: Ask “Why might I have chosen this?” instead of “Why me?”
  • Relationships: See difficult people as teachers, not enemies.
  • Decision-Making: Treat life as a curriculum—choose growth over stagnation.
  • Spiritual Practice: Use meditation or journaling to connect with your inner guide.
  • Compassion: Forgive yourself and others—we’re all learning.

Overall Takeaway: Life is a classroom, death is a return home, and everything in between is part of our soul’s evolution.
