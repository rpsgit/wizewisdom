Map of Consciousness

Hawkins maps human states from ~1–1000. Below 200 drains energy and fosters reactivity; above 200 empowers growth, and 500+ opens to love, joy, and peace. Use this as a practical tool for daily self-check.

1. Below 200 — Weakness & Suffering

Shame, guilt, apathy, fear, anger, pride dominate. Life feels reactive and heavy. If you notice these states, name the feeling and avoid feeding it further.

2. 200–499 — Personal Power & Growth

Starting with courage (200), you move into responsibility, willingness, acceptance, and reason—practical states for productivity, relationships, and resilience.

3. 500+ — Higher Consciousness

Love, joy, peace, and enlightenment. These levels are expansive and healing; daily practices like gratitude and compassion help you stabilize here.

How to use it?

  1. Check your state: ask, “Am I below or above 200 right now?”
  2. If below 200: pause, name the emotion (“I notice shame/fear”), breathe, and choose one small value-based action.
  3. If above 200: lean into it—practice habits that deepen clarity (journaling, mindful action, service).

Takeaway: Spend more moments above 200 (courage)—that’s where true freedom, health, and authentic living begin.
