My Big TOE

by Thomas Campbell

Core Idea

Campbell, a physicist and consciousness explorer, proposes that reality is fundamentally made of consciousness, not matter. What we call the “physical world” is a kind of virtual reality, generated within a larger system he calls the Larger Consciousness System (LCS).

His “TOE” explains everything—physics, metaphysics, spirituality—under one framework: we are individuated units of consciousness evolving toward love (lower entropy).

Key Insights

  • Consciousness is Primary: Matter is secondary, like pixels on a screen. Your awareness is the real “player.”
  • Life as a Simulation: Physical reality is a training ground for consciousness to evolve.
  • Entropy and Love: Lowering personal entropy (becoming more ordered, less selfish) = growing in love.
  • Free Will is Central: Choice is the engine of growth; every decision shapes your consciousness.
  • Multiple Realities: Dreams, out-of-body experiences, and non-physical realities are just different data streams within the LCS.

How to Apply Daily

  • Make Choices Toward Love: Ask, “Am I acting out of fear, ego, or out of care and growth?”
  • Reduce Entropy: Simplify life where possible—less chaos, more clarity, healthier systems.
  • Meditate: Quiet the mind to access deeper levels of consciousness and intuition.
  • Treat Life as Practice: Every interaction is an opportunity to evolve—especially difficult ones.
  • Be Open to Experience: Don’t dismiss dreams, intuition, or “strange” perceptions—they may be data from beyond the physical.
  • Live with Responsibility: Recognize that your choices ripple through the consciousness system.

Overall TakeawayMy Big TOE reframes life: you’re not just surviving in a random universe—you’re a consciousness evolving in a meaningful system. Every moment and choice is part of your growth toward love and higher awareness.
